20th Century Studios


20th Century Studios, Inc.(formerly Twentieth Century Fox Film Corporation)
is an American film studio that is a subsidiary of Walt Disney Studios, a division of The Walt Disney Company.
The studio is located on the Fox Studio Lot in the Century City area of Los Angeles.
Walt Disney Studios Motion Pictures distributes and markets the films produced by 20th Century.
